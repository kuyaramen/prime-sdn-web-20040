## Content Rewriting Guidelines

### Principles
1. **Public-Friendly Language**: Convert bureaucratic language to accessible, engaging content
2. **Benefit-Focused**: Emphasize citizen benefits and outcomes
3. **Action-Oriented**: Use active verbs and clear calls-to-action
4. **Timeline Context**: Include relevant timeline phases (2028, 2031, 2034, 2037, 2040)
5. **Progress Indicators**: Include KPI targets and current status where applicable

### Example Transformation

**Original (Roadmap):**
"Province-wide HRIS and eDMS operational across all provincial departments and half of component LGUs."

**Rewritten (Website):**
"Transforming Government Operations: Our province-wide Human Resource Information System (HRIS) and Electronic Document Management System (eDMS) are now operational across all provincial departments, streamlining workflows and improving service delivery for citizens."
